Welcome — if you arrived here from one of my courses, you&rsquo;re in the right place. This organization hosts the hands-on side of the teaching: starter templates to build on, example projects, and the demos we work through in class, organized by course.

The best starting point is your course page on [teaching.andrewoshei.com](https://teaching.andrewoshei.com) — it links the repos you need alongside the syllabus, slides, and assignments, and carries the current session schedule and announcements.

Not one of my students? You&rsquo;re welcome to browse and borrow. These materials are course-sized introductions to mobile development — iOS/Swift and Android/Kotlin — kept current as living teaching resources.
